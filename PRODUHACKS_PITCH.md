### Slide 2: The Problem 🚩
- **Headline:** Numbers don't tell the whole story.
- **Bullet Points:**
    - Wearables track *what* (HR, SpO2) but lack the *why*.
    - High-latency AI feels robotic and dangerous for medical triage.
    - Anxiety rises when you have to wait 3+ hours for an urgent care visit.

### Slide 3: The Solution 🧪
- **Headline:** A Multimodal "Life Loop".
- **Concept:** An AI agent that **sees** your symptoms, **hears** your concerns, and **understands** your telemetry in real-time.
- **Key Visual:** (Link to Dashboard Screenshot)

### Slide 4: Key Feature: Multimodal Consultation 👁️👂
- **Headline:** The power of **Gemini 2.0 Flash**.
- **Description:** Real-time Vision (Camera for skin/symptom check) and Voice (Empathy-driven AI Doctor).
- **Benefit:** Immediate triage with sub-2s latency.

### Slide 5: Key Feature: Deterministic Safety 🛡️
- **Headline:** Math over Hallucinations.
- **Description:** We decoupled medical risk scoring from the LLM. 
- **Proof:** 100% accuracy in vital categorization (Stable/Monitor/Critical) using standardized clinical protocols (AHA/ADA).

### Slide 6: Market Opportunity 📈 (For Spring VC)
- **Headline:** The Personal Healthcare Revolution.
- **Target:** Seniors, busy professionals, and rural communities.
- **Scalability:** Move from a single-user app to a remote-monitoring platform for whole families or clinics.

### Slide 7: Technical Stack 🛠️
- **Frontend:** Next.js 14, Tailwind v4
- **Backend:** Node.js, Express, WebSockets
- **AI Core:** Google Gemini 2.0 Flash (Multimodal Reasoning)

### Slide 8: Future Vision 🗺️
- **Headline:** From Triage to Treatment.
- **Roadmap:** Integration with Apple HealthKit, AI-driven medication reminders, and direct clinician hand-off.
